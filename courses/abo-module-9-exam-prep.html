Test-Taking Strategies

The NOCE is 125 multiple-choice questions in 2 hours (about 1 minute per question). Read each question carefully. Eliminate obviously wrong answers first. Don't change answers unless certain. Mark difficult questions and return to them.

Key InsightUse process of elimination. If you can eliminate 2 options, you have a 50% chance even if guessing between the remaining two.

Day Before & Test Day

Day before: Light review only, get good sleep, prepare ID and confirmation. Test day: Arrive 15-30 min early, bring valid photo ID, no phones in testing room. During exam: Use all time available, review flagged questions, trust your preparation.

Key InsightDon't cram the night before. Your brain consolidates learning during sleep. A rested mind performs better than an exhausted one with slightly more information.
